Waakye

Description

A traditional Ghanaian dish made with rice and beans, often served with spaghetti, boiled eggs, and shito.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup black-eyed peas
  • Waakye leaves or baking soda
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil beans until tender.
  2. Add rice and waakye leaves to the pot.
  3. Cook together until rice is done and water is absorbed.
