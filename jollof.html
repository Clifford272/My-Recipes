Jollof Rice

Description

A West African rice dish cooked in a flavorful tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes and onion.
  2. Fry the mix in oil, add seasoning.
  3. Add rice and water, cook until soft.
