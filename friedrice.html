Fried Rice

Description

A savory dish with stir-fried rice, vegetables, and protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • Carrots and peas
  • 2 eggs
  • Chicken or shrimp
  • Soy sauce
  • Onion and garlic

Steps

  1. Cook vegetables and meat.
  2. Scramble eggs and mix in.
  3. Add rice and soy sauce, stir well.
